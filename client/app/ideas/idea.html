   Tope Fasua Education 12 January 2014

Boko Haram has been getting in the way of education in the country due to their tendency to kidnap girls. This has caused a painful decline in the willingness of many women to go to school.

Once upon a time, there was a country where people did not have to worry about anything. All they had to worry about was putting food on their tables, getting their salaries and paying their children's school fees. How about we all move to a parallel universe where Boko Haram does not exist?

One other thing that we could do is to explore the many-worlds hypothesis and simply take a different path that would launch us into a different trajectory.

About BuildNigeria

BuildNigeria was created to be the platform where the best minds and forward thinking Nigerians can contribute well-thought-out ideas toward rebuilding our great country, Nigeria.

We set out to build this platform upon the realization that a lot of Nigerians are increasingly becoming passionate and interested in national development, and thanks to social media, actively express their thoughts and ideas on platforms like Facebook and LinkedIn.

The problem however is that it is hard to coordinate these ideas (from different people on different platforms) for the citizens and most importantly the leaders who would ultimately act on the best ideas/solutions. BuildNigeria aims to solve this problem.
